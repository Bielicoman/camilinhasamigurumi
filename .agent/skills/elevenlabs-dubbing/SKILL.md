# ElevenLabs Dubbing

Automatically dub videos into 29+ languages with natural voices.

## Features

- **Multi-language dubbing** – Dub into any supported language
- **Voice preservation** – Maintain original speaker characteristics
- **Lip-sync ready** – Audio timed for video sync
- **Fast turnaround** – Minutes instead of days

## Common Use Cases

- Expanding video reach to new markets
- Creating localized educational content
- International video marketing
- YouTube/TikTok content localization
- Podcast multi-language editions

## How It Works

1. Upload video or provide video URL
2. Select target language(s)
3. Choose voice preferences
4. Get dubbed video with synced audio

## Setup

Requires ElevenLabs API key and video file.
